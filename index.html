Model Architecture Insights

LSTM – Long Short-Term Memory networks excel at learning long-range dependencies in sequential data, making them a robust baseline for stock price prediction across volatile markets.

Transformer – Attention mechanisms allow the Transformer to weigh all past time steps simultaneously, often capturing complex volatility patterns and macro correlations more effectively.

GRU – Gated Recurrent Units are a streamlined RNN variant that trains ~30% faster than LSTM with comparable accuracy, ideal for rapid re-training on fresh data.

StockAI Pro

Advanced AI-powered stock price forecasting using LSTM, GRU, and Transformer neural networks with real-time technical analysis, portfolio tracking, and AI sentiment analysis.

🧠

Deep Learning Models

Three architectures — LSTM, GRU, and Transformer — trained on 60+ days of historical data with RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, SMA, and EMA features predict next-day closing prices.

📡

Data Sources

Yahoo Finance, Alpha Vantage, and Quandl APIs. All sources are merged, normalized, and checked for outliers before model input to ensure high-quality predictions.

⚙️

Preprocessing

Z-score outlier removal, seasonal decomposition, missing data imputation, MinMax normalization, and strict time-split validation to prevent data leakage.

📏

Evaluation

Models graded on RMSE, MAE, MAPE, and R² on held-out test sets. The Model Compare page benchmarks all three architectures with full statistical analysis.

💼

Portfolio Tracker

Track your holdings, monitor P&L, view AI-powered next-day projections for your portfolio, and get rebalancing suggestions based on model confidence scores.

📰

AI News Sentiment

Simulated market news feed with NLP-based sentiment scoring. Each article is tagged Bullish / Bearish / Neutral with confidence score. Aggregate sentiment feeds into AI signal.
